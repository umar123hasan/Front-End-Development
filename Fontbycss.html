KAWASAKI Z900

The Kawasaki Z900 is a powerful and aggressive middleweight naked bike known for its thrilling performance and sharp “Sugomi” styling. It is equipped with a 948cc inline-four engine that produces around 123 horsepower, offering a smooth yet punchy ride ideal for both street and spirited weekend rides. The 2025 model comes with significant upgrades, including a 6-axis IMU that powers advanced electronics like cornering ABS, traction control, power modes, cruise control, and a bi-directional quickshifter. It also features a 5-inch TFT display with Bluetooth connectivity and voice-assisted navigation through the Rideology app.
